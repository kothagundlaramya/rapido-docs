# How to Book a Ride in Rapido App

## Step 1: Install the Rapido App
- Go to Play Store or App Store
- Search for "Rapido"
- Install the app

## Step 2: Login
- Open the app
- Enter your mobile number
- Verify OTP

## Step 3: Enter Pickup and Drop Location
- Enter your pickup point
- Enter your destination

## Step 4: Choose Ride Type
- Bike
- Auto
- Cab

## Step 5: Confirm Booking
- Check fare
- Click on "Book Ride"
